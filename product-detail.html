About This Expression

The Cellar Supreme 30 (Cellar Supreme 30) represents the absolute pinnacle of the LongGuoYan portfolio. Drawing on 30 years of careful cellar aging in the mineral-rich clay cellars of Maotai Town, this expression embodies the full depth, complexity, and imperial grandeur that defines the finest Jiangxiang sauce-aroma baijiu.

The bottle design — finished in imperial gold with hand-applied dragon motifs — reflects the spirit within: bold, refined, and worthy of the most significant occasions. Whether presented as an exceptional gift or savoured at a banquet table, the Cellar Supreme 30 commands attention.

Tasting Profile

Appearance Crystal clear, luminous with a faint golden hue; legs run slow and graceful down the glass
Nose Deep, layered sauce-aroma with toasted grain, dried plum, honey, and aged oak. Decades of cellaring reveal a bouquet of extraordinary complexity
Palate Full-bodied and silky smooth. Waves of roasted grain, dark chocolate, osmanthus and a whisper of dried mushroom. The mouthfeel is round, warming, with no harsh edges
Finish Exceptionally long and warming. The sauce-aroma lingers with subtle sweetness and a dry, satisfying warmth that lasts minutes after the last sip
Serve Best served neat at room temperature in a tulip-shaped glass. Allow 5–10 minutes to breathe before serving

The Craft Behind the Spirit

01
Selection of Raw Materials

Only premium red-glutinous sorghum grown in the red-soil highlands of Guizhou, combined with pure spring water from the Chishui River watershed, forms the foundation of every batch.

02
Triple-High Fermentation (San Gao Gong Yi)

High-temperature Daqu preparation, high-temperature stacking fermentation, and high-temperature distillation — the defining process of authentic Jiangxiang baijiu that creates its signature deep sauce aroma.

03
Seven Rounds of Distillation

The mash undergoes seven separate distillation rounds across a full year production cycle, with each round yielding base spirits of distinct character that will be blended by the master craftsmen.

04
30-Year Cellar Aging

The finest base spirits are sealed in century-old Maotai Town clay cellar jars, undisturbed for three decades. Time, clay minerals, and the unique microclimate of Maotai Town transform raw spirit into a transcendent expression.

05
Master Blending

Senior blending masters with decades of experience carefully select and blend aged spirits from different cellars and years to achieve the precise Cellar Supreme 30 profile — rich, balanced, and unforgettable.

Food Pairing Guide

The Cellar Supreme 30's deep sauce-aroma and full body pair beautifully with rich, umami-forward dishes. Its bold character can hold up to strong flavours while its silky texture complements more delicate fare.

🥩
Braised & Roasted Meats

Red-braised pork belly, Peking duck, and roasted lamb shoulder are ideal companions — the spirit's depth mirrors the richness of slow-cooked meats

🦞
Premium Seafood

Steamed lobster, abalone, and whole fish in black bean sauce — the spirit cleanses the palate between each bite

🍄
Earthy Dishes

Truffles, mushroom hotpot, and aged tofu pair well with the earthy, umami notes of a long-aged Jiangxiang

🍫
Dark Chocolate

High-cocoa dark chocolate (70%+) bridges the toasted grain notes beautifully — an unexpected but elegant pairing
