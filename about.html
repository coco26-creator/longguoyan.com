Company Profile

Guizhou Long Guo Yan Baijiu Co., Ltd. is located in Maotai Town, Renhuai City, Guizhou Province — the heartland of China's finest sauce-aroma baijiu. Situated along the banks of the Chishui River, the distillery benefits from an exceptional natural environment and century-old traditional brewing techniques to craft a uniquely distinctive sauce-aroma spirit.

The company was rebuilt upon the foundation of an ancient traditional distillery and established as a public-private enterprise. Shaped through generations of master craftsmen, it has grown into a celebrated Guizhou heritage brand.

1998–2016: Long Guo Yan Distillery, Maotai Town, Renhuai, Guizhou

2016–Present: Guizhou Long Guo Yan Baijiu Co., Ltd. — full brand upgrade, expanding into international markets with sauce-aroma culture.

We hold firm to our core philosophy: "Inherit ancient techniques, brew with mastery, quality above all." Using Maotai Town's unique microbiological environment and pure Chishui River water, we strictly follow the "triple high" process — high-temperature starter preparation, high-temperature accumulation fermentation, and high-temperature distillation — through 7 rounds of spirit extraction and a minimum of 5 years of cellar ageing, before a bottle of Long Guo Yan is complete.

Brewing Craft

Our Brewing Process

◆
🌾

Finest Ingredients

We strictly select premium locally-grown glutinous red sorghum from Guizhou, with plump grains and an amylopectin content of over 98%, laying the structural foundation for our sauce-aroma spirit.

🔥

Triple-High Process

We adhere to the triple-high technique — high-temperature starter preparation, high-temperature accumulation fermentation, and high-temperature distillation — creating the complex and distinctive aroma profile of our sauce-aroma baijiu.

🏺

Five-Year Ageing

Through 7 rounds of spirit extraction and a minimum of 5 years of cellar ageing, the spirit fully esterifies, resulting in a richer, smoother taste and a more enduring, lingering aroma.
