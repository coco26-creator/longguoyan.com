Company Profile

Guizhou LongGuoYan Baijiu Co., Ltd. is located in Maotai Town, Guizhou Province — the heartland of China's finest sauce-aroma Jiangxiang baijiu. Rebuilt upon the foundation of an ancient traditional distillery, it has grown into a celebrated Guizhou heritage brand.

1958: Founded as a traditional distillery in Maotai Town

2016–Present: Full brand upgrade, expanding into international markets.

We hold firm to our core philosophy: "Inherit ancient techniques, brew with mastery, quality above all." We strictly follow the "triple high" process and a minimum of 5 years of cellar ageing.

Brewing Craft

Our Brewing Process

◆
STEP 01
Finest Ingredients

We strictly select premium locally-grown glutinous red sorghum from Guizhou, the foundation of every exceptional bottle.

STEP 02
Triple-High Process

Adhering to high-temperature starter preparation, high-temperature fermentation, and high-temperature distillation — the soul of Jiangxiang.

STEP 03
Five-Year Ageing

A minimum of 5 years of cellar ageing in traditional earthen jars, resulting in a richer, smoother, deeply layered taste.

STEP 04
Master Blending

Our master blenders artfully combine aged spirits from different vintages, achieving a harmonious balance of aroma, taste, and finish.

STEP 05
Rigorous Quality Control

Every batch undergoes strict multi-stage sensory and laboratory testing before bottling — ensuring only the finest reaches you.

STEP 06
Artisan Bottling

Hand-inspected and carefully packaged, each bottle of LongGuoYan is a tribute to 66 years of unbroken craft tradition.
