About Us

Our Heritage

◆

Guizhou Longguo Banquet Baijiu Co., Ltd. is located in Maotai Town, Renhuai City, Guizhou Province — the heartland of China's finest Jiangxiang sauce-aroma baijiu. Blessed with an exceptional natural environment and time-honored traditional brewing techniques, we craft a uniquely characteristic Jiangxiang spirit.

Rebuilt upon the foundation of an ancient traditional distillery, the brand has been shaped through generations of master craftsmen, and has grown into a celebrated Guizhou heritage brand — perfectly blending Eastern culture with the essence of baijiu.

Early PRC Era
Rebuilt on traditional distillery foundations as a public-private enterprise
1998–2016
Longguo Banquet Distillery, Maotai Town, Renhuai, Guizhou
