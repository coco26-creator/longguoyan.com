About Us

Our Story

◆

Guizhou Long Guo Yan Baijiu Co., Ltd. is located in Maotai Town, Renhuai City, Guizhou Province — the heartland of China's finest sauce-aroma baijiu. Blessed with an exceptional natural environment and time-honored traditional brewing techniques, we craft a uniquely characteristic sauce-aroma spirit.

Rebuilt upon the foundation of an ancient traditional distillery, the brand has been shaped through generations of master craftsmen, and has grown into a celebrated Guizhou heritage brand — perfectly blending Eastern culture with the essence of baijiu.

Early PRC Era
Rebuilt on traditional distillery foundations as a public-private enterprise
1998–2016
Long Guo Yan Distillery, Maotai Town, Renhuai, Guizhou
2016–Present
Guizhou Long Guo Yan Baijiu Co., Ltd. — full brand upgrade, going global
